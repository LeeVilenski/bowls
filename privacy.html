Privacy Policy

Short Mat Bowls — Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Short Mat Bowls is a single-player / local multiplayer game. It runs entirely in your browser or on your device. No account is required and no personal data is collected or transmitted.

Data we collect

We collect no personal data whatsoever. Specifically:

  • No names, email addresses, or contact information
  • No location data
  • No device identifiers
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No advertising identifiers

Local storage

The game may store settings (such as your preferred bowl colour or number of ends) in your device's local storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us.

Third-party services

The game does not use any third-party SDKs, analytics services, or advertising networks.

Children

This game is suitable for all ages. Because we collect no personal data, there are no special considerations for users under 13.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date.

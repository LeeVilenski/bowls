Privacy Policy

Short Mat Bowls — Last updated: June 2026

Overview

Short Mat Bowls can be played solo against the AI, locally with a friend, or online against other players. No account or sign-up is required. When playing offline, no data leaves your device. When playing online, the minimal data described below is transmitted to enable matchmaking and gameplay.

Data we collect

We do not collect:

  • Email addresses or contact information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage analytics or telemetry
  • Advertising identifiers

Online play

When you use the online multiplayer feature, the following data is processed:

  • Display name — the name you choose is sent to our matchmaking server so other players can see it in the lobby. This is a free-text name you provide; no real name is required.
  • Game data — bowl positions, jack placement, shot timing, and other in-game moves are transmitted between players to keep the game in sync.
  • Connection data — IP addresses are exchanged between players as part of the peer-to-peer (WebRTC) connection used for real-time gameplay. This is inherent to how WebRTC works and is not logged by us.

Game and lobby records are stored on our server while a game is in progress and may be retained for a short period afterwards for reconnection purposes. We do not use this data for any purpose other than running the game.

Local storage

The game stores settings in your device's local storage, including your preferred bowl colour, number of ends, and your chosen display name for online play. This data never leaves your device except when your display name is sent to the matchmaking server during online play (as described above).

Third-party services

Online play relies on the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — hosts the game lobby and stores moves for asynchronous play. Subject to the Supabase Privacy Policy.
  • PeerJS — brokers the initial peer-to-peer connection between players. Subject to the PeerJS terms. Once the connection is established, game data flows directly between players.

No analytics services or advertising networks are used.

Children

This game is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. The only user-provided information is an optional display name for online play, which should not contain real personal information.

Changes to this policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be published at this URL with a revised date.
